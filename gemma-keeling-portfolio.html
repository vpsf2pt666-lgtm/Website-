About
Gemma

I'm Gemma — an Irish lawyer and incoming NYU School of Law student based in New York. I graduated with First Class Honours in Law (LL.B.) from Trinity College Dublin in 2024, where my capstone thesis examined algorithmic audiencing and freedom of expression online.

Most recently I worked as a Legal Clerk at McCarney Law P.C. in New York, drafting briefs, pleadings, and discovery materials across a high-volume civil litigation practice. Before that, I interned at Macguill and Company in Dublin in criminal defence.

Outside of law I'm a board member of the Green Economy Foundation in West Cork, a competitive sailor, and I keep a personal archive of writing and photography.
